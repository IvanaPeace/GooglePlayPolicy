Privacy Policy for AR Fun

Effective Date: [16/04/2025]

AR Fun was developed as part of a university project and is designed to provide fun and educational augmented reality experiences for students in the classroom.

1. Personal Information

This app does not collect, use, or store any personal information from users. This app does not ask for names, email addresses, or other identifying information.

2. Camera and Device Access

AR Fun uses your device’s camera and motion sensors to enable AR features (like placing objects or detecting images). This data is only used in real-time on your device and is not saved or shared.

3. Children’s Privacy

AR Fun is made for educational use and may be used by students of all ages, younger student should get guidance from a teacher or parent supervision.

  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not include advertising
  • Does not include in-app purchases

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not do not use any third-party services that collect data from users.

5. Data Security

This app does take data privacy seriously. Since this app does not collect any user data, there is nothing stored or transmitted to protect.
